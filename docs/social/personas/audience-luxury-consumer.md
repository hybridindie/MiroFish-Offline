# Persona: Luxury Consumer

**Persona ID:** PRS-AUD-LUXURY-CONSUMER-001
**Persona Type:** Audience
**Demographic:** 25-45, High income ($100K+), Urban/Suburban, US/UK/EU
**Estimated Population:** ~25M US households

## Psychographic Profile

- Quality over quantity mindset
- Brand-conscious but not brand-loyal
- Invests in experiences over things
- Research-intensive purchaser (reads reviews, compares)
- Values exclusivity and scarcity
- Comfortable with premium pricing for premium quality

- High-end travel (boutique hotels, experiences)

## Discovery Triggers

1. **Instagram**: Visual discovery, brand accounts, influencer content
2. **Word of mouth**: Social circle recommendations
3. **Editorial content**: Vogue, Harper's Bazaar, luxury publications
4. **YouTube**: In-depth reviews, "best of" content
5. **Direct brand experience**: Events, stores, concierge

## Brand Engagement

- Responds to exclusivity and limited availability
- Values storytelling and brand heritage
- Skeptical of mass-market influencer partnerships
- Prefers subtle over loud luxury positioning
- High lifetime value, strong brand loyalty when convinced
- Will pay premium for service and experience
