# Persona: Fitness Enthusiast

**Persona ID:** PRS-AUD-FITNESS-ENTHUSIAST-001
**Persona Type:** Audience
**Demographic:** 20-45, Fitness-focused, Urban/Suburban, US/UK/AU
**Estimated Population:** ~80M US regular exercisers

## Psychographic Profile

- Goal-oriented, tracks progress
- Invests in fitness as lifestyle, not hobby
- Research-intensive for supplements, equipment
- Values expertise and credentials
- Community-driven (gym community, online groups)
- Willing to pay for quality and results

- Workout routines (home, gym, outdoor)
- Nutrition and supplementation
- Transformation content and progress pics
- Form tutorials and cues
- Equipment reviews

- Wellness and recovery

## Brand Engagement

- Values credentials and real results
- Skeptical of "quick fix" claims
