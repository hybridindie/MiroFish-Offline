# Persona: Gen Z Urban Consumer

**Persona ID:** PRS-AUD-GENZ-URBAN-001
**Persona Type:** Audience
**Demographic:** 16-26, US/UK/BR/ID, Urban/Suburban
**Estimated Population:** ~80M (US + UK + BR major metros)

## Psychographic Profile

- Digital-native, platform-agnostic content consumption
- Values authenticity over polish (imperfect > corporate)
- Socially conscious, supports values-aligned brands
- Comfortable with both free and paid content
- Short attention span but deep engagement when invested
- FOMO-driven discovery (if friend shares, must see)

## Content Affinities

- Beauty (skincare, makeup, nail art)
- Fashion (thrift, dupes, outfit inspo)
- Gaming (mobile, indie, esports)
- Music (new releases, fandoms, covers)
- Social commentary (politics-lite, drama)
- Mental health (normalized, not stigmatized)
- Food (recipes, restaurant reviews, Mukbang)

## Discovery Triggers

1. **Friend share** (direct message): Most trusted, drives 30-40% of discovery
2. **TikTok For You Page**: Algorithmic discovery, 40-50% of content consumed
3. **Instagram Reels**: 20-30% of discovery for style/beauty
4. **YouTube Shorts**: Growing, especially for how-to content
5. **Reddit**: Niche communities, less algorithm-controlled

## Brand Engagement

- Responds to micro-influencers (10K-100K) more than macro
- Values brand authenticity and social stance
- Will call out "corporate" or "inauthentic" brands publicly
- Supports brands that support causes (but skeptical of performative activism)
- High engagement with limited drops/exclusives
