# Persona: Millennial Parent

**Persona ID:** PRS-AUD-MILLENNIAL-PARENT-001
**Persona Type:** Audience
**Demographic:** 28-40, Parents with kids under 12, Suburban, US/UK
**Estimated Population:** ~45M US households

## Psychographic Profile

- Time-poor, seeks efficiency in content consumption
- Research-driven purchasing decisions
- Values recommendations from trusted sources
- Comfortable with paid content for time savings
- Balancing screen time concerns with entertainment needs
- Nostalgic (grew up with early internet, remembers dial-up)

## Platform Usage

| Platform | Usage Frequency | Primary Activity | Content Type |
|----------|---------------|------------------|--------------|
| Facebook | Daily 30-60 min | Parenting groups, local | Groups, Watch |
| YouTube | Daily 1-2 hrs | How-to, entertainment | Long-form, Shorts |
| Instagram | Daily 30-60 min | Inspiration, shopping | Reels, Stories |
| TikTok | Daily 30 min-1 hr | Quick tips, entertainment | Short video |
| Pinterest | Weekly 2-3x | Planning, recipes, DIY | Pins, boards |

## Content Affinities

- Parenting hacks and tips
- Quick meal planning (30-min meals)
- Product reviews (baby gear, toys, household)
- Kids activities and entertainment
- Home organization/DIY
- Family travel on a budget
- Educational content for kids
- Mental health (postpartum, work-life balance)

## Discovery Triggers

1. **Parent groups/Facebook Groups**: Highly trusted, drives 30-40% of product discovery
2. **YouTube reviews**: In-depth, research before purchase
3. **Instagram**: Visual inspiration, shopping features
4. **Word of mouth (playgroups)**: Offline trusted recommendations
5. **Pinterest**: Planning, aspirational content

## Brand Engagement

- Responds to "real parent" influencers over polished content
- Values practical over aspirational
- Skeptical of "perfect parent" imagery
- High conversion from authentic recommendations
- Responds to deals, bundles, and value propositions
- Willing to try new brands if recommended by trusted sources
