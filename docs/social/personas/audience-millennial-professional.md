# Persona: Millennial Professional (25-40)

**Persona ID:** PRS-AUD-MILLENNIAL-001
**Persona Type:** Audience
**Demographic:** 25-40, US/UK/BR, Suburban/Urban, Household Income $60-200K
**Estimated Population:** ~100M (US + UK)

## Psychographic Profile

- Time-poor, convenience-driven
- Values quality over quantity
- Comfortable with subscriptions (multiple, often 5-10)
- Research-driven for high-ticket purchases
- Work-life balance conscious
- Values "me time" and self-care
- Established brand loyalty when trust earned

## Content Affinities

- Personal finance (investing, mortgages, retirement)
- Home improvement/DIY
- Parenting/family content
- Health and fitness (realistic, sustainable)
- Cooking/recipes (quick, meal prep)
- Travel (experiences, not budget)

## Discovery Triggers

1. **Google search**: Primary research for high-ticket (home, finance)
2. **YouTube reviews**: Deep dive before purchase (tech, fitness)
3. **Instagram ads**: Emotional appeal, lifestyle aspiration
4. **Facebook groups**: Community recommendations (parenting, local)
5. **Podcasts**: Thought leadership, industry insights
6. **Email marketing**: Deal alerts, loyalty rewards

## Brand Engagement

- Loyal to brands that deliver consistent quality
- Will pay premium for convenience and time savings
- Shares honest reviews (positive and negative)
- Values good customer service
- Recommendations within trusted networks (friends, family)
- Less influenced by "viral" trends, more by utility
