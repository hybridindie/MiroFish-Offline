## Persona Structure

Each persona includes:

- **Platform Presence**: Primary/secondary/supplement platforms with follower ranges
- **Content Themes**: Top content categories and topics
- **Monetization Paths**: Tiered revenue streams
- **Cross-Platform Journey**: Typical creator/audience journey across platforms
- **Audience Segments**: Sub-segments within the persona
- **Platform-Specific Notes**: Algorithmic and cultural nuances per platform
- **Key Metrics**: Engagement, conversion, and revenue estimates (need validation)
- **Source Platform Packets**: Related platform research packets
