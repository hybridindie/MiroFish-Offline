# Persona: Tech Professional

**Persona ID:** PRS-AUD-TECH-PROFESSIONAL-001
**Persona Type:** Audience
**Demographic:** 22-45, Tech-employed or tech-interested, Urban, US/UK/Remote
**Estimated Population:** ~30M US tech workers + 50M tech-interested

## Psychographic Profile

- Early adopter mentality
- Values efficiency and productivity
- Research-driven, reads specs and reviews
- Comfortable with digital products/services
- Career-focused, invests in skills
- Skeptical of marketing, values substance

## Platform Usage

| Platform | Usage Frequency | Primary Activity | Content Type |
|----------|---------------|------------------|--------------|
| LinkedIn | Daily 30-60 min | Professional content, news | Posts, articles |
| YouTube | Daily 1-2 hrs | Tutorials, reviews, learning | Long/short video |
| X/Twitter | Daily 30-60 min | Tech news, discussions | Threads, links |
| Reddit | Daily 30-60 min | Community discussions | Subreddits |
| Hacker News | Daily 30 min | Tech news, startups | Links, comments |

## Content Affinities

- Product reviews (hardware, software)
- Programming/coding tutorials
- Tech industry news and analysis
- Productivity tools and workflows
- AI and emerging technology
- Startup/venture ecosystem
- Career advice and negotiation
- Privacy and security

## Audience Segments Within

| Segment | Role | Primary Interest | Content Need |
|---------|------|------------------|--------------|
| Software Engineer | Dev | Coding, tools, tutorials | Technical depth |
| Product Manager | PM | Strategy, tools, leadership | High-level + detail |
| Tech Leadership | Exec | Strategy, trends, hiring | Business focus |
| Tech-Interested Consumer | Other | Products, reviews | Accessible content |
| Developer/Tinkerer | Hobby | DIY, home lab, open source | Technical, hands-on |

## Discovery Triggers

1. **YouTube**: Reviews, tutorials, deep dives
2. **Reddit**: Community discussions, AMAs
3. **X/Twitter**: Tech news, influencer threads
4. **LinkedIn**: Professional content, industry thought leadership
5. **Hacker News**: Early access to news, startup buzz

## Brand Engagement

- Values expertise and technical depth
- Skeptical of shallow content or marketing
- High conversion on tools that improve productivity
- Newsletter and content creator subscriptions common
- Responds to detailed reviews over polished ads
- Community-driven (Reddit, Discord for dev tools)
