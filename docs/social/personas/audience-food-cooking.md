# Persona: Food & Cooking Enthusiast

**Persona ID:** PRS-AUD-FOOD-COOKING-001
**Persona Type:** Audience
**Demographic:** 22-55, Passionate about food, Global, US/UK/EU primary
**Estimated Population:** ~100M US "food interested"

## Psychographic Profile

- Values quality ingredients and preparation
- Enjoys the process, not just the result
- Social aspect of food (cooking for others)
- Experiments with new cuisines/techniques
- Comfortable spending on quality cookware
- Food media heavy consumers

## Platform Usage

| Platform | Usage Frequency | Primary Activity | Content Type |
|----------|---------------|------------------|--------------|
| YouTube | Daily 1-2 hrs | Recipe tutorials, channels | Long/short video |
| TikTok | Daily 30-90 min | Quick recipes, food trends | Short video |
| Instagram | Daily 30-60 min | Food photography, discovery | Reels, Posts |
| Pinterest | Weekly 2-3x | Recipe saving, planning | Pins, boards |
| Reddit | Weekly 2-3x | Recipe discussions | Cooking subreddits |

## Content Affinities

- Recipe tutorials (all cuisines)
- Cooking techniques and skills
- Kitchen equipment and gear
- Ingredient sourcing and quality
- Restaurant reviews and recommendations
- Food science and "why it works"
- Meal prep and batch cooking
- Baking and pastry

## Discovery Triggers

1. **YouTube**: Recipe videos, channel subscriptions
2. **TikTok**: Quick recipes, food trends
3. **Pinterest**: Recipe saving, meal planning
4. **Instagram**: Food photography, restaurant discovery
5. **Friends/Family**: Recipe sharing, dinner invitations

## Brand Engagement

- Values execution over presentation
- Responds to "real" home cooking, not overly polished
- Kitchen equipment brand loyal once convinced
- Recipe content converts on ingredients and tools
- Cookbooks and online courses popular
- High engagement with local/farmers market content
