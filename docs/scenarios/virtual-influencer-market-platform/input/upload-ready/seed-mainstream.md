## Policy Summary

- Explicit sexual content prohibited on IG, TikTok, and YouTube.
- TikTok realistic AI depictions require AIGC labeling.
- YouTube requires AI disclosure in realistic/news contexts.
- IG/TikTok/YouTube strike systems can pause monetization before account removal.
- Monetization thresholds:
  - TikTok: 10K followers + 100K views in 30 days + 18+
  - YouTube YPP: 1K subscribers + 4K watch hours or 10M Shorts views
