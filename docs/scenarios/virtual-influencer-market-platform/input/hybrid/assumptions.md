# Hybrid Track Assumptions

## Fixed assumptions

- Scenario horizon weeks: 12
- Simulation rounds: 120
- Creative output per week: 10 short videos, 18 text-native updates, 8 community prompts
- Team capacity: 1 strategist, 1 editor, 1 community manager
- Paid support budget range USD: $2,500-$5,000/month (X/Reddit community management tooling; premium community platform fee; limited paid amplification for discovery posts)

## Audience assumptions

- Target age bands: 18-44
- Language: English
- Community depth weighted higher than pure reach

## Measurement assumptions

- Visibility window: first 72 hours post publish
- Growth baseline: follower plus community participation trend
- Monetization window: conversion and retention over 90 days
