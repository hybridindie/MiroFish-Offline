# Mainstream Policy Notes

## Platforms in scope
- Instagram Reels
- TikTok
- YouTube Shorts

## Checklist

1. Content policy constraints
- [x] Sexual content policy captured — All three platforms prohibit explicit sexual content. Suggestive-but-not-explicit is permitted. AI/virtual personas are not banned; TikTok AIGC (AI-Generated Content) label is mandatory for realistic synthetic depictions (2024 policy); YT AI disclosure required for AI-generated realistic video used in news or political context; IG has no mandatory AI label for entertainment content as of Q1 2026.
- [x] Harassment and safety policy captured — IG: progressive warning then restriction; TikTok: immediate takedown for severe violations, 3-strike system for repeat offences; YT: 3-strike Community Guidelines system leading to channel termination.
- [x] Synthetic media disclosure policy captured — TikTok: AIGC label mandatory for realistic depictions; IG: Paid Partnership / Branded Content label required for sponsored posts; YT: disclosure required for AI-generated realistic video in news context. No platform currently mandates AI persona identification for pure entertainment accounts.

2. Monetization policy constraints
- [x] Partner program requirements captured — TikTok Creativity Program: 10K followers + 100K views in past 30 days + 18+ age (US only); IG Reels Bonuses: invite-only as of 2025, shifting to brand-partnership-first model; YT Partner Program: 1,000 subscribers + 4,000 watch hours or 10M Shorts views in past 12 months.
- [x] Ad suitability constraints captured — TikTok Brand Safety Inventory Filter (BSIF) active; IG reduces ad inventory for content with policy flags even if not removed; YT Moderate content rating limits advertiser pool; all three demonetize content touching sensitive topics (mental health, conflict, adult-adjacent themes) even if not TOS-violating.
- [x] Regional payment restrictions captured — UK: no platform-specific restrictions beyond standard banking; US: Form W-9 (domestic) or W-8BEN (foreign) required by all three platforms; EU payors: VAT handled automatically through Meta/Google/ByteDance platform infrastructure.

3. Operational constraints
- [x] Account health strike logic documented — IG: 3 strikes (30-day window) before restriction; TikTok: 3 strikes result in permanent ban; YT: 3 Community Guidelines strikes in 90 days result in channel termination. Monetization can be paused independently of removal.
- [x] Appeal process documented — IG: Appeals via Help Center → review queue (7-14 day average); TikTok: In-app appeal, 5-7 day average, human review available for monetisation-affecting decisions; YT: YouTube Studio appeal link, 7-30 day average, human review available for YPP monetization decisions.
- [x] Escalation contacts documented — IG Business Support: available for creator accounts with 10K+ followers via Meta Business Suite; TikTok Creator Support via TikTok One creator portal; YT Creator Support live chat available for YPP members in YouTube Studio.
