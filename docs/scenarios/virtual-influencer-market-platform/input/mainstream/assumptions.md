# Mainstream Track Assumptions

## Fixed assumptions

- Scenario horizon weeks: 12
- Simulation rounds: 120
- Creative output per week: 14 short videos, 21 story or post fragments
- Team capacity: 1 strategist, 1 editor, 1 community manager
- Paid support budget range USD: $3,000-$6,000/month (TikTok Spark Ads + IG Reel boosting; affiliate tool subscriptions; Creator Studio management tooling)

## Audience assumptions

- Target age bands: 18-34 primary
- Language: English
- Safety and moderation policy: conservative mainstream-safe baseline

## Measurement assumptions

- Visibility window: first 72 hours post publish
- Growth baseline: follower and engagement trend over 12 weeks
- Monetization window: proxy conversion and retention over 90 days
